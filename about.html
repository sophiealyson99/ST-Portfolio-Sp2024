About Me

Hello! I’m Sophie, aspiring public librarian and nature enthusiast with 4 years of combined public and academic library experience. Currently enrolled in Drexel University’s Master’s of Library Science (MLIS) program, I aspire to become a public librarian dedicated to empowering others in their use of libraries as a resource of both learning and community. My educational background is in Geography, with a focus on justice, ethics, and diversity in space. This dedication to safe spaces is what originally brought me to public libraries in 2020, when I found my passion for public library service.
